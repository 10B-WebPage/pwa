It is a video game in which our protagonist Kenji along with his exploration team decide to go to a very popular place within their community where they said there were apparitions from another world, some with skepticism and others with fear, they still decide to go to create a good content within their blogs on the internet that makes them famous and thus get a little more money.

At first Kenji did not believe in all those things, but as time goes by and due to certain events that occur once he crosses the doors, he begins to wonder if there really is a door on the other side? Can tragic events involve demonic energy? Who was responsible?

As he continued to find a way out and help his friends, one by one, they disappeared or even lost their lives. With hope as their final resource, they will long to get out of there as soon as possible and try to forget what happened inside that hell on earth.

But they did not count on the fact that the Umbra only wanted one specific person, that since he felt his energy by just stepping at the entrance of the house and feeling his energy, this specter was sure of his past and present revenge that perhaps he would finally once and for all he could carry out in order to free his soul in pain and thus obtain the justice that was never given to him.

There are only two important enough questions left to resolve, will Kenji be able to survive along with his friends or will he have to sacrifice valuable things to get out of there alive? Will Umbra get the expected result of his plan?
